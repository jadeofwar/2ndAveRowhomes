Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 1, 2025

2nd Avenue Rowhomes ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting the personal information you share with us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or communicate with us.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us, including:

  • Name
  • Phone Number
  • Email Address
  • Inquiries regarding apartment availability

We also automatically collect non-personal information such as your IP address, browser type, and browsing patterns to improve our website experience.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • To respond to your inquiries about our properties.
  • To schedule tours and process rental applications.
  • To send you updates regarding availability or pricing (only if requested).
  • To improve our website and customer service.

3. SMS Communications

By initiating a text message or providing your phone number, you verify that the number provided is your mobile number and you consent to receive text messages from 2nd Avenue Rowhomes regarding your inquiry. Message and data rates may apply.

No mobile information will be shared with third parties/affiliates for marketing/promotional purposes. All the above categories exclude text messaging originator opt-in data and consent; this information will not be shared with any third parties.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to others. We may share generic aggregated demographic information not linked to any personal identification information with our business partners and trusted affiliates for the purposes outlined above.

5. Cookies

Our website may use "cookies" to enhance user experience. You may choose to set your web browser to refuse cookies or to alert you when cookies are being sent.

6. Your Rights

You may request to review, update, or delete your personal information by contacting us. To opt-out of SMS communications, reply STOP to any message you receive.
